Bias Beacon Profile Flow

Evidence should lead to informed use, not passive observation.

01

Evidence

Linked cases, dates, rulings, and source references establish trust before interpretation begins.

02

Pattern Context

Compare a judge or agency against county and state baselines to see whether conduct is ordinary or outlying.

03

Implication

Plain-language interpretation explains what the pattern means in practice for litigants, defendants, and communities.

04

Action Path

Open related cases, save materials to workspace, or move into drafting and research without losing the source trail.

The Watchtower

A control system, not a landing page.

The Watchtower is the command layer: domain selectors, county filters, line charts, distribution views, and short summaries that point directly to the next drill-down.

Workspace

Research, drafting, and document use stay separate from public data.

The workspace layer is reserved for uploads, document organization, assistant workflows, and drafting support. The public record remains the public record.
