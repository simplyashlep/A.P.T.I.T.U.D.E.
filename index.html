Our Mission

A.P.T.I.T.U.D.E. transforms Oregon's public justice records into actionable intelligence. We believe that when behavior becomes data, data becomes pattern, pattern becomes evidence, and evidence becomes accountability.

Our platform reveals the hidden architecture of justice: where disparities emerge, where institutional patterns repeat, and where accountability can be restored.
