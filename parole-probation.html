Community Supervision & Corrections

Probation, parole, revocation, and the back-end trajectory of supervision and reentry.

Community supervision represents the largest component of correctional control in the United States. Probation and parole supervision decisions affect over 100,000 Oregonians. This section examines supervision practices, revocation patterns, and their impact on communities and individuals.
