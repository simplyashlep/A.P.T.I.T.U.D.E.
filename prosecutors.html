Key Insight

Prosecutorial discretion is the single most consequential decision point in the criminal justice system. Charging choices, plea offers, and sentencing recommendations shape outcomes more than any other actor.

County District Attorney Offices

Oregon's 36 DA Offices

Each of Oregon's 36 counties has an elected District Attorney who leads the prosecutorial office. These offices vary dramatically in size, resources, and approach.

Multnomah County DA

Portland Metro · Population: 812,000
Large Metro
~15,000 Annual Filings
92% Conviction Rate
38% Plea Rate

Largest DA office in Oregon. Oversees prosecution for the Portland metropolitan area, including complex homicide, drug trafficking, and public corruption units.

Where This Data Comes From

OJC Oregon Judicial Department

Primary source for case filing and disposition data across all 36 counties.

ODA Oregon District Attorneys Association

Provides aggregate statistics, policy positions, and training standards for DA offices statewide.

CJC Criminal Justice Commission

Publishes annual reports on sentencing practices, racial disparity, and recidivism by county.

LEDS Law Enforcement Data System

Records of arrests, charges, and case dispositions used for cross-referencing prosecutorial data.
