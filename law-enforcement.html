Law Enforcement Agencies

Police stops, searches, arrests, and the front-end interactions that set the trajectory for system involvement.

Law enforcement represents the first contact point in the criminal justice system. Police stops, searches, and arrests disproportionately affect certain populations. This section provides data on police activity across Oregon's agencies, focusing on equity and outcome disparities.
